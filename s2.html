Chapter 2

The Race Begins

The rabbit laughed even harder at the turtle's words. "You think being steady is better than being fast? Let's have a race then! From this big oak tree to the river. We'll see who wins!"

This is going to be the easiest race I've ever run! I might even take a nap!

— The Rabbit, overconfident as ever

All the animals in the forest gathered to watch. The fox agreed to be the referee. The owl would watch from the trees to make sure no one cheated.

"This is going to be hilarious!" said the squirrel. "The turtle doesn't stand a chance!"

The turtle simply nodded. "I accept your challenge, Rabbit. Let the best animal win."

The fox marked the starting line under the big oak tree and the finish line at the riverbank. The race was about to begin!

🏁 The Starting Line

The rabbit stretched his long legs, feeling confident and proud. He looked at the turtle and smirked. "See you at the finish line... eventually!"

The turtle took a deep breath, focusing on the path ahead. He knew he was slow, but he also knew that he never gave up.

Fox (referee): "On your marks... Get set... GO!"

The rabbit shot forward like an arrow, kicking up dust behind him. Within seconds, he was far ahead, leaving the turtle far behind.

The turtle began his slow but steady pace. Step by step, he moved forward, never stopping, never slowing down.

⚡ The Rabbit's Confidence

The rabbit was so far ahead that he couldn't even see the turtle anymore. "This is too easy!" he thought. "I'm so far ahead, I could take a nap and still win!"

And that's exactly what he decided to do. The rabbit found a nice shady spot under a tree, yawned, and fell fast asleep.

🐢 The Turtle's Determination

Meanwhile, the turtle kept moving. One slow step after another. He passed the tree where the rabbit was sleeping but didn't stop or slow down. He just kept going, focused on the finish line.

The animals watching whispered among themselves. "Look! The turtle is passing the sleeping rabbit! Should we wake him?"

But no one dared. They watched as the turtle slowly but surely made his way toward the river.

Who will reach the finish line first? Will the rabbit wake up in time?

Vocabulary

Overconfident
Too confident; having too much faith in one's abilities
Determination
Firmness of purpose; the quality that makes you continue trying
Steady
Regular, even, and continuous in development

Think About It

  • Why did the rabbit decide to take a nap during the race?
  • What can we learn from the turtle's approach to the race?
  • Have you ever seen someone succeed through steady effort rather than quick talent?
  • What do you think will happen next? (The story continues to the moral...)

The Moral

"Slow and steady wins the race."

The rabbit's overconfidence led him to underestimate his opponent, while the turtle's steady determination kept him moving forward.
